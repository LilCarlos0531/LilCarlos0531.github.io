ABOUT ROBOTICS

Robots are cool!

Robotics is an interdisciplinary branch of computer science and engineering. Robotics involves design, construction, operation, and the use of robots. What is a robot? Well robots are the product of the robotics field, where programmable machines are built that can assist humans. Robots were originally constructed to handle tedious tasks (like building cars on an assembly line), but have since expanded well beyond their original uses to perform other tasks like, cleaning homes and assisting with incredibly complex surgeries. Each robot has a differing level of independence, ranging from a remote-controlled bot that carries out tasks that a human has full control over to fully-autonomous bots that perform tasks without any external influences.

Welcome to my website. My name's Carlos Hernandez. Now let's talk about some different types of robots that contribute to our society. There are generally 5 different types of robots pre-programmed robots, humanoid robots, autonomous robots, teleoperated robots, and augmenting robots. Pre-programmed robots operate in a controlled environment where they do simple, tedious tasks. Its job is to perform tasks longer, faster and more efficiently than a human. Humanoid robots are robots that look human and mimic human behavior. These robots usually perform human-style activities and are sometimes designed to have human faces and expressions. Autonomous robots operate independently. These bots do things often without human supervision. They are quite unique because they use sensors to “see” the world around them, and then employ decision-making skills to take the next step based on their data and mission. Teleoperated robots are semi-autonomous bots that use a wireless network to allow remote control from a safe distance. These robots usually work in extreme geographical conditions, weather, circumstances, etc. Finally, Augmenting robots either enhance current human capabilities or replace the capabilities a human may have lost they’re pretty much prosthetic limbs or exoskeletons used to lift hefty weights.
