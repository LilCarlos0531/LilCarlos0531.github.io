Jobs in Robotics

#1 Electromechanical technicians translate schematics and diagrams. They identify the proper method to assemble machines and other technological and electronic equipment; they have a national average salary of $54,264 per year.

#2 Robotics account managers handle many financial and sales responsibilities within the industry. They have a national average salary of $60,267 per year.

#3 Aerospace engineers may help develop the artificial intelligence of robots as well as design robots that can efficiently perform tasks such as drilling, painting and building aircraft like drones. They have a national average salary of $103,162 per year.

#4 Robotics engineers are responsible for designing, developing, testing,and building robots. Robotics engineers often collaborate closely with software developers to create highly impressive robots that can perform certain tasks properly. They have a national salary of $96,137 per year.

#5 Computer scientists in the robotics field, they contribute to the development of artificial intelligence applications that can help humans and robots interact effectively. Computer scientists test these systems very regularly and determine whether they function properly. They have a national salary of $106,430 per year

#6 Software engineers build the computerized systems that exist inside of robots and help to ensure robots are able to efficiently carry out their duties. They have a national average salary of $110,601 per year
